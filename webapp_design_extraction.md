# Webapp Design Extraction

## 1. Overall Visual Identity

- **Theme:** Dark-mode SaaS landing page / product marketing site.
- **Primary background:** Near-black / black throughout the full page.
- **Accent color:** Bright aqua / cyan-green used for:
  - eyebrow label near the hero title
  - primary CTA button
  - small highlights / graph accents
  - footer brand accent
- **Secondary colors:**
  - white / off-white for main headings
  - muted gray for supporting copy
  - dark gray borders, dividers, panels, and card outlines
- **Aesthetic:** Minimal, premium, technical, AI analytics / enterprise software style.
- **Density:** Spacious vertical rhythm with generous section padding.
- **Shape language:** Mostly rectangular cards with subtle rounding; thin borders; low-contrast separators.

---

## 2. Page Composition

The page is a **single long landing page** composed of the following major sections in order:

1. Top navigation bar
2. Hero section with left-aligned headline and CTA buttons
3. Four-column feature summary strip
4. Large product screenshot / dashboard preview
5. Customer / brand logo strip
6. Analytics feature showcase with 2x2 card grid
7. Benefits + supported AI platforms section (two-column layout)
8. FAQ accordion section
9. Customer stories / case studies teaser row
10. Secondary CTA section with two featured article cards
11. Large footer with multi-column links and company details

---

## 3. Canvas / Layout Width

- The page sits inside a **centered content container**.
- Content width appears constrained to a typical SaaS desktop max width.
- Most sections align to a common horizontal grid.
- Hero content is left-aligned inside the container.
- Visual showcase sections use large centered cards that nearly span the content width.
- Footer returns to a structured multi-column grid.

## 15. Design System Notes

### Typography
- Sans-serif modern UI typeface.
- Strong hierarchy:
  - very large bold hero heading
  - medium section headings
  - small captions and helper text
  - tiny nav and footer text
- White text on black for major headings.
- Gray secondary text for all explanatory copy.

### Buttons
- Rounded rectangles.
- Primary: aqua filled with dark text.
- Secondary: dark/gray filled or outline.
- Small button size in resource cards and nav.

### Borders and Dividers
- Thin, soft gray lines.
- Used extensively to separate:
  - sections
  - FAQ rows
  - AI platform rows
  - feature grid cells
  - screenshot containers

### Cards / Panels
- Dark charcoal surfaces over black page background.
- Slight rounding.
- Minimal shadow if any.
- Emphasis comes from contrast and spacing, not heavy elevation.

### Icons / Graphs
- Data visualization motifs are central to the page.
- Charts use white/gray structure with occasional aqua or bright highlight points.
- UI chips/pills are used in product demonstrations.
